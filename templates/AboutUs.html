About Us

We are a group of final year students from the Department of Artificial Intelligence & Data Science at CMR Institute of Technology, Bangalore. Our passion for solving real-world problems through data-driven technology inspired us to develop this Diabetes Prediction platform. This website leverages machine learning models to help users assess their risk of diabetes based on medically relevant parameters. It is designed to promote early awareness and empower individuals to seek timely medical guidance. By combining our technical skills and academic knowledge, we aim to contribute towards smarter and more accessible healthcare solutions.
